Services: Offering a full-range of retouching and restoration services.

Typical projects include:

  • Scanning and restoring old photographs
  • Cleaning up images from digital photo shoots: catalog, still life, wedding, etc.
  • Beauty retouching, such as fashion and modeling shoots
  • Real-estate: remove power lines and other unwanted objects, adjust lighting
  • Fine-art retouching: cleaning scratches and lint.

Pricing: Pricing depends heavily on the services needed and the number of photographs. Contact us with a rough outline of the project for a quote.
