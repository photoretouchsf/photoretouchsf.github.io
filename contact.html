About Kristen and Photo Retouch SF:

Kristen Sard is a photo retouch artist, a Certified Adobe Expert in Photoshop, and a photographer.

Born and raised in New York City, she attended Parson's School of Design and received her BFA in Photography in 1989. She has exhibited her work in galleries in New York and San Francisco.

Inspired by the beauty of the west coast, Kristen moved with her husband to San Francisco where they currently live, work, and are proud parents of their son.
